About Cachelot

Founded by Luke Hawash, Cachelot is revolutionizing sperm donor recruitment through data-driven strategies, AI-powered outreach, and seamless partnerships with the nation's leading fertility clinics. Our mission is to make donor recruitment more accessible, efficient, and transparent for everyone involved.

By combining big data with personalized outreach, we streamline the process for donors and fertility centers alike—reducing wait times, optimizing lead quality, and ultimately shaping the future of family building.

How It Works

1. Identify

We scrape and analyze data to find potential donors meeting key criteria: age, health, education, and more.

2. Outreach

Our AI-driven system reaches out with personalized messaging—making the first step to donation simple and clear.

3. Sign Up

Interested donors complete a quick online form and an eligibility quiz to confirm they fit the baseline requirements.

4. Referral

Cachelot refers qualified donors to partner clinics for final testing and approval, ensuring a seamless transition.

5. Earn & Save Lives

Donors receive compensation once approved—while helping families in need of healthy, reliable sperm donations.
