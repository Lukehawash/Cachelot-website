How It Works

1. Identify

We leverage data scraping and analytics to find potential donors that match our core criteria (age, health, education).

2. Outreach

AI-driven messaging engages each prospect with personalized emails and DMs, making the donation process clear and accessible.

3. Sign Up

Qualified individuals complete an online form, confirming basic eligibility and capturing key information.

4. Referral

We refer approved leads to partner clinics, handling the logistics and ensuring a seamless hand-off.

5. Bonus & Impact

Once a donor completes the clinic's approval, they earn a bonus while helping families in need worldwide.

Partners

Cachelot provides sperm banks and fertility clinics with highly-qualified, pre-screened donor leads, reducing your recruitment costs and accelerating time to approval. Our AI-driven approach ensures a constant flow of potential donors, freeing you to focus on patient care.

About Cachelot

Founded by Luke Hawash in 2024, Cachelot aims to revolutionize the fertility industry with a simple yet powerful idea: connecting ideal donors with clinics nationwide via a seamless, technology-forward platform. Our mission is to reshape donor recruitment by integrating advanced data science, AI-driven outreach, and empathetic human touch.

Whether you're a potential donor or a clinic partner, Cachelot's streamlined funnel, cutting-edge lead scoring, and nationwide reach ensure that everyone benefits from our data-driven innovation.

Tech & Data

Our platform is built on secure cloud infrastructure, with integrated CRMs, advanced data-scraping algorithms, and large language models for personalized outreach. We employ industry-standard security protocols to ensure that sensitive data remains confidential and in compliance with relevant regulations.

From automated follow-ups to user-friendly dashboards, Cachelot harnesses AI at every stage to optimize donor engagement and clinic workflow. The result? Higher conversion rates, satisfied donors, and a more efficient fertility industry.
